num_pickedup_LimeLime: the number of Lime scooters picked up during the time a Lime scooter remained idle
num_pickedup_LimeBird: the number of Lime scooters picked up during the time a Bird scooter remained idle
num_pickedup_BirdLime: the number of Bird scooters picked up during the time a Lime scooter remained idle
num_pickedup_BirdBird: the number of Bird scooters picked up during the time a Bird scooter remained idle
